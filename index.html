About Our University

Excellence Through Innovation

Founded in 1975, our university has been at the forefront of academic excellence and innovation for over 50 years. We pride ourselves on fostering an environment where cultural diversity and athletic achievement go hand in hand with academic success.

The annual ELAAN festival represents our commitment to providing students with a holistic educational experience that celebrates creativity, teamwork, and competitive spirit.

About ELAAN 2025

ELAAN, our flagship cultural and sports festival, has been a cornerstone of university life for over two decades. The festival brings together thousands of participants from universities across the country in a celebration of talent, skill, and camaraderie.

This year's theme, "Unity in Diversity," celebrates the rich cultural tapestry that makes our university community unique while emphasizing the unity that sports and cultural activities foster among students from diverse backgrounds.
